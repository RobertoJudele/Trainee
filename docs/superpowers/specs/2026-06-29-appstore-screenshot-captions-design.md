# App Store Screenshot Captions & Order — Design

**Date:** 2026-06-29
**App:** Trainee (fitness trainer marketplace)
**Scope:** Titles, subtitles, and ordering for the App Store listing screenshots.

## Decisions

- **Primary audience:** Clients (people searching for a trainer). The trainer side
  appears once, at the end, as a teaser.
- **Caption language:** Romanian (App Store Romania).
- **Tone:** Informal *tu* — matches the in-app Romanian copy. Copy is benefit/CTA-led
  (ad-creative pass), not just descriptive.
- **Ordering strategy:** Approach 1 — "Pure client funnel" for positions 1–4 (lead with
  the marketplace, build trust, then proximity), followed by a 3-shot trainer mini-story
  at positions 5–7. All 7 screenshots are used.
- **Accuracy constraints (App Store):** No claim that the platform *verifies* trainers or
  their credentials — it does not. Certifications are self-uploaded; reviews are genuine
  client reviews. No "book/reserve" claim — there is no in-app booking; clients contact
  the trainer via Instagram/Facebook/WhatsApp. "Free" is framed as the real 1-month trial.

## Rationale

1. **Position 1 (Search)** sells the core promise in the slot that matters most — the
   search-result preview thumbnail. Communicates "marketplace of rated, priced trainers"
   at a glance.
2. **Position 2 (Profile + packages)** answers "can I judge them?" with pricing and
   package transparency.
3. **Position 3 (Reviews + certs)** is the trust anchor — the trainer's uploaded
   certifications plus genuine client reviews let the user judge. No platform-verification
   claim (see accuracy constraints). Placed third, while interest is high.
4. **Position 4 (Map)** delivers the proximity/discovery payoff — "it's local." No booking
   claim; the app surfaces nearby trainers, contact happens off-platform.
5. **Positions 5–7 (trainer mini-story)** flip audience: the schedule screen hooks with
   "Ești antrenor?", the daily planner shows day-level client assignment, and the home hub
   closes with the free-trial CTA. Placed last so they don't dilute the client lead.

## Notes for production

- Captions are sized for a standard 2-line overlay.
- Diacritics must be preserved (ă, â, î, ș, ț).
- Apply the same order and captions across all required device sizes (6.7", 6.5", 5.5"
  as App Store Connect requires).
